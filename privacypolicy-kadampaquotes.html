Privacy Policy for Kadampa Quotes

Effective Date: July 2025

App Developer: Piccolo Studio LTD

App Name: Kadampa Quotes

Package Name: com.piccolostudio.kadampaquotes

Introduction

Kadampa Quotes is a spiritual application offered as a service to the New Kadampa Tradition and followers of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso. This app provides daily Buddhist teachings and quotes from Geshe Kelsang Gyatso, primarily from "The Meditation Handbook," which are openly available online and published by Tharpa Publications.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to your use of the Kadampa Quotes app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Data Collection and Usage

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • User behavior tracking

Local Data Storage Only

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • App Settings: Theme preferences and display settings
  • Reminder Configurations: Your custom reminder times, selected topics, and enabled/disabled status
  • Quote of the Day: Daily quote selection for consistent daily experience
  • App State: First launch status and recent quotes for app functionality

All this data is stored locally using your device's secure storage and is never transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for functionality:

Notifications Permission

  • Purpose: To send you spiritual reminders and daily quotes at your chosen times
  • Usage: Only used for local notification scheduling
  • Data: No notification data is collected or transmitted

Schedule Exact Alarm (Android)

  • Purpose: To ensure precise timing of your spiritual reminders
  • Usage: Only for local alarm scheduling
  • Data: No alarm data is collected or transmitted

Third-Party Services

No Third-Party Analytics

We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or data collection frameworks.

No Advertising

The app contains no advertisements, ad networks, or advertising-related code.

No External Data Transmission

The app does not transmit any data to external servers, APIs, or third-party services.

Content and Intellectual Property

Quote Sources

The quotes featured in this app are from the teachings of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso, primarily from "The Meditation Handbook" published by Tharpa Publications. These teachings are:

  • Openly available online
  • Published for public benefit
  • Used with respect for their spiritual purpose

Content Attribution

All quotes are properly attributed to Geshe Kelsang Gyatso and the New Kadampa Tradition. The app serves as a tool to make these teachings more accessible to practitioners.

Data Security

Local Storage Security

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is stored using secure, encrypted local storage methods
  • No data leaves your device

No Network Transmission

  • The app does not require internet connectivity
  • No data is transmitted over networks
  • No external API calls are made

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages and contains only spiritual content appropriate for family use.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

Clear App Data

  • Clear all app data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Reset app settings within the app

Control Notifications

  • Enable or disable notifications at any time
  • Customize reminder times and topics
  • Delete individual reminders

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

App Purpose and Mission

Kadampa Quotes is created as an offering of service to the New Kadampa Tradition and followers of Venerable Geshe Kelsang Gyatso. Inspired by a wish to repay the great kindness of this precious Spiritual Guide, this app aims to be a helpful tool for supporting practitioners in daily life.

Through gentle reminders featuring the words of this Meditation Master, we hope many people will be connected to the meaning of Dharma throughout their day. The app is provided free of charge as a spiritual service to the community.
